Technical Architecture

Layer 1: Hardware

Smart Glasses Platform: Affordable AR glasses (<‚Çπ4,000) with embedded camera, display, speakers, and solar panels. Lightweight design (under 80g) for all-day comfort.

Layer 2: Edge AI Processing

On-Device Intelligence: Qualcomm Snapdragon XR chip runs computer vision, AR rendering, and AI translation locally. No cloud dependency ensures privacy and offline capability.

Layer 3: Content Management

Pre-loaded Heritage Database: High-resolution 3D models, historical data, and multilingual audio content stored locally. Regular updates via WiFi sync at site offices.

Layer 4: AR Experience

Real-time Rendering: SLAM technology for spatial tracking, object recognition for monument identification, and dynamic AR overlay compositing at 60fps for smooth experience.

Implementation Phases

Phase 1: Pilot Sites (Current)

Deployed at 5 heritage sites in Telangana. Gathering user feedback and optimizing hardware/software based on real-world usage patterns.

Phase 4: Global Heritage

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do the smart glasses cost?

Our smart glasses are priced below ‚Çπ4,000, making them one of the most affordable AR solutions for heritage tourism. Rental options are also available at participating sites for as low as ‚Çπ100 per visit.

Do I need internet connectivity to use MIRAI ARXR?

No! MIRAI ARXR is designed to work 100% offline. All content, including 3D models, translations, and audio narration, is pre-loaded on the device. This makes it perfect for remote heritage sites with limited connectivity.

Which languages are supported?

We currently support 10+ Indian languages including Hindi, English, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Bengali, Gujarati, and Punjabi. We're continuously adding more regional languages based on user demand.

How can heritage sites partner with MIRAI ARXR?

We work with Archaeological Survey of India (ASI), state tourism boards, and private heritage site management. Contact us through the form above or email partnerships@miraiarxr.com to discuss collaboration opportunities.

Is the technology difficult to use?

Not at all! MIRAI ARXR is designed for all age groups and tech literacy levels. The interface uses intuitive gestures and voice commands. Most users are comfortable within 2-3 minutes of starting their tour.

What about data privacy?

Privacy is our priority. All AI processing happens on-device (edge computing). No personal data is collected or transmitted. Your experience is completely private and secure.

How long does the battery last?

With integrated solar panels, our glasses provide up to 8 hours of continuous use in normal conditions. This is more than enough for a full day of heritage site exploration.

Can I purchase glasses for personal use?

We're currently focused on site-based rentals to ensure content quality and device maintenance. However, we're exploring consumer retail options for 2025. Join our waitlist by contacting us!
